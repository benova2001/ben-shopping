About Ben Shopping

Our Mission

To connect Tanzanian products with the world, empower local sellers, and create a platform where people shop, connect, and grow every day.

Our Vision

We aim to be the leading digital market for Tanzanian culture, creativity, and commerce — locally and globally.

Why Choose Ben Shopping?

  • Secure mobile money payment options (Yas,Airtel Money,HaloPesa,M-Pesa).
  • Direct support via WhatsApp and Live Chat.
  • Next-day delivery in most regions.
  • We support local sellers & small businesses.

Our Journey

  • 2024:Idea born during a search for local market solutions.
  • 2025:Website launched with full mobile payment options.
  • Future:Mobile app & AI product recommendation system
